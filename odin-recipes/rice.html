Rice

The Ultimate Side-dish!

This staple literally goes with anything! Including our Air Fryer Chicken recipe! This side dish is always a healthy addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 Cups of Rice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Rice Cooker

Instructions

  • Fill Rice Cooker with 3 cups of rice
  • Fill rice cooker with water, meeting the 3 cup indicator inside machine
  • Add Salt
  • Add Pepper
  • Add a spoonful of Butter
  • Power on Rice Cooker machine and wait for it to beep. Indicating it is complete
  • Enjoy!
