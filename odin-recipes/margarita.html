Margaritas!

My Go-to Drink!

Girllllll... Wherever it is I go, whether it be the club, a restaurant, or even when I'm home with the girls, I drink a margarita. If I have the option to add flavor, I always do. But even a generic, ice cold margarita on the rocks can never do me wrong. And the best part is, you can easily make it at home! C'mon sis lets get a drink!!

Ingredients

  • Ice
  • Sour Mix
  • Tequila of your choice
  • Triple Sec
  • Lime for garnish

Instructions

  1. Fill glass with ice
  2. Add 2oz of Tequila
  3. Add 1oz of Triple Sec
  4. Add 1 oz of Sour Mix
  5. Shake well and garnish with lime
